Shaping Impact Through Policy & Practice

A glimpse into my professional values, journey, and vision for sustainable development in Zambia and beyond.

About Me

I am Wanjivwa Sinkala, a Zambian development economist and operations professional passionate about creating impactful solutions in both public and private sectors. Over the past 7 years, I have cultivated expertise in administration, procurement, compliance, risk management, and program evaluation across development, banking, retail, and legal sectors. My work focuses on promoting institutional integrity, results-oriented strategies, and inclusive growth.

Education

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Development Studies with Economics from the University of Zambia, where I gained a strong foundation in economic theory, development policy, and project planning. This program provided me with the tools to analyze social and economic challenges, understand governance and policy issues, and propose practical development solutions.

I further specialized with a Postgraduate Diploma in Monitoring & Evaluation Methods, also from the University of Zambia. This program equipped me with practical skills to design monitoring frameworks, evaluate project outcomes, analyze data, and provide evidence-based recommendations for improving program effectiveness and sustainability.

To enhance my strategic and policy-making expertise, I completed a Master of Arts in Economic Policy Management at the University of Zambia. Here, I developed advanced skills in economic policy analysis, fiscal and monetary policy, public sector management, and designing strategies to drive inclusive growth and sustainable development.

Professional Skills

  • Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E): Designing and implementing evaluation frameworks for programs and projects, including USAID-funded initiatives.
  • Risk Management & Compliance: Ensuring adherence to policies, mitigating operational risks, and strengthening internal controls.
  • Procurement & Operations Management: Managing supply chains, vendor relationships, and process optimization.
  • Data Analysis & Reporting: Transforming data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.
  • Stakeholder Engagement & Coordination: Collaborating effectively with government agencies, NGOs, SMEs, and community groups.
  • Project & Policy Analysis: Evaluating initiatives, recommending improvements, and supporting evidence-based policy development.

Key Projects & Impact

  • USAID Alternatives to Charcoal (A2C) Evaluation: Led a process evaluation assessing the effectiveness of clean energy alternatives, providing actionable recommendations to improve program implementation and sustainability.
  • Financial Inclusion Initiatives: Supported SMEs and individuals through risk assessment and credit access programs, enhancing economic empowerment and growth.
  • Reforestation & Environmental Projects: Participated in tree-planting initiatives with PAMZ, Standard Chartered Bank Zambia, Care International Zambia, and FNB Zambia, promoting environmental sustainability.
  • Retail & Operations Optimization: Managed administrative and operational processes at Jumbo Wholesale and Jet Supermarket Zambia, improving efficiency and compliance.

Professional Values

My professional ethos is grounded in accountability, transparency, and inclusive growth. I am committed to advancing financial inclusion, sustainable livelihoods, and the empowerment of individuals and small businesses.

Beyond Work

Beyond professional commitments, I engage in thought leadership by sharing insights on economic policy, program evaluation, and sustainable development. I participate in forums and discussions that shape best practices and innovative approaches for development, financial inclusion, and environmental sustainability.
