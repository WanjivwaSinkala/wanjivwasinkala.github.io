About Me

I am Wanjivwa Sinkala, a Zambian development economist and operations professional passionate about creating impactful solutions in both public and private sectors. With over 7 years of experience, I have cultivated expertise in administration, procurement, compliance, risk management, and program evaluation.

Education

Bachelor of Arts in Development Studies with Economics – University of Zambia.

Postgraduate Diploma in Monitoring & Evaluation Methods – University of Zambia.

Master of Arts in Economic Policy Management – University of Zambia.

Professional Skills

  • Monitoring & Evaluation: Implementing frameworks for program effectiveness.
  • Risk Management & Compliance: Strengthening internal controls and reducing operational risks.
  • Procurement & Operations: Managing vendors, logistics, and processes efficiently.
  • Data Analysis: Turning data into actionable development insights.
  • Stakeholder Coordination: Engaging with public, private, and civil society actors.

Key Projects & Impact

  • USAID A2C Evaluation: Led process evaluation for clean energy alternatives.
  • Financial Inclusion: Expanded SME credit access and empowerment.
  • Reforestation: Worked with PAMZ, FNB, Standard Chartered on sustainability projects.
  • Retail Operations: Enhanced compliance at Jumbo Wholesale and Jet Supermarket.

Professional Values

Accountability, transparency, and inclusive growth define my approach. I strive to promote institutional integrity and empower local communities.
