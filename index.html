WELCOME TO MY PROFESSIONAL PORTFOLIO

I am Wanjivwa Sinkala, a Development Economist, Policy Analyst, and M&E Practitioner.

I’m a Zambian development economist passionate about using evidence and data to shape inclusive policies and sustainable growth. With over 7 years of experience in administration, financial services, and development programs, I’ve supported projects that enhance financial inclusion, strengthen monitoring and evaluation systems, and promote data-driven decision-making.

My work bridges economic research and practical policy implementation — ensuring that insights from data translate into measurable impact. I collaborate with public institutions and development partners to deliver strategies that build resilient economies and empower communities.
