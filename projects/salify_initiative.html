Salify Credit Access (Upcoming Initiative – 2026)

Salify Credit Access (SCA) is an upcoming financial inclusion initiative planned for 2026. Its mission is to provide individuals, SMEs, and underserved communities in Zambia with access to affordable, transparent, and flexible financial solutions.

The initiative focuses on developing customer-centered loan assessment procedures, implementing effective risk management frameworks, and ensuring compliance with national financial regulations. By leveraging digital platforms and innovative service delivery, SCA aims to streamline lending operations and strengthen financial literacy among clients.

Expected outcomes include expanded financial inclusion, enhanced operational efficiency, and increased opportunities for entrepreneurship and income generation. Salify Credit Access is designed to combine technology-driven innovation with responsible lending practices to contribute to Zambia’s economic growth.
