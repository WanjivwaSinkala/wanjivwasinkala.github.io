Tree Planting & Reforestation Initiative

As part of community and environmental conservation efforts, I contributed to a tree planting and reforestation project in collaboration with Standard Chartered Bank Zambia, PAMZ, Care International Zambia, FNB Zambia, and other partners.

This initiative aimed to combat deforestation, enhance urban green spaces, and raise awareness about climate resilience among local communities.

My role included:

  • Coordinating community engagement and awareness activities
  • Collaborating with stakeholders to organize tree planting drives
  • Supporting monitoring and evaluation of tree survival rates
